Why We Race Against Time

Every year, millions of perfectly good products reach their expiry dates and are destroyed. Fashion items are shredded. FMCG products are dumped. All while people need them and businesses lose revenue.

StockShift was born from a simple idea: What if we could see expiry not as an endpoint, but as a deadline for action? What if every product had a visible countdown, pushing us to act before it's too late?

We're not just a marketplace. We're a rescue mission. Every product you see here is racing against time. Your purchase doesn't just get you a great deal‚Äîit completes a rescue operation.

How The Rescue Works

Early Detection

We identify products 60-90 days before expiry, giving maximum time for rescue operations.

Urgency Amplification

As expiry approaches, products get promoted more aggressively with countdown timers.

Smart Matching

We connect expiring products with buyers who need them most, from charities to discount retailers.

Last Resort Rescue

If products reach critical stage, they're automatically offered to recycling/composting partners.
